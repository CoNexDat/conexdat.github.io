Tools and software developed by the group:

- **[LaNet-vi](http://lanet-vi.fi.uba.ar/)** — large-network visualization
  via *k-core* decomposition.
- **[I'm Here!](http://lanet-vi.fi.uba.ar/i_am_here)** — interactive
  visualization of the Internet topology showing where the user is.
- **TiX (Traffic information eXchange)** — a tool to measure the quality
  of residential Internet access.
- **ANTop** — ad-hoc routing protocol, simulated under
  [QUENAS](http://sourceforge.net/projects/quenas).
