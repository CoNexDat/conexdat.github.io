    Data Science

    Large-scale analysis of Twitter traces, electoral data, and Call Detail Records (CDR). We infer human mobility, content demand, and online community dynamics.

    We combine complex-network theory with machine-learning techniques to extract structure and meaning from massive digital footprints:

    • Political networks on Twitter during the 2015 and 2019 Argentinian elections — we detect the dynamic formation of opinion via the community structure of hashtag semantic networks.
    • Human mobility from Call Detail Records (CDR) and geolocated social-media data; regularity and socio-economic stratification.
    • Bias, fairness and values in digital platforms — predicting demographics, moral foundations, and attitudes (e.g. towards vaccination) from online behaviour.
